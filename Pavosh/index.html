ABOUT ME

I'm a music producer and DJ from Mexico City producing melodic bass music since 2018.

My sound is defined by UK Dubstep influences, and other many genres like Melodic Riddim Colour Bass and Bass House.

I stepped into the spotlight with my remix of Skrillex’s track ‘Slats Slats Slats’ and I'm also have been remixing other artists like Madeon, Barely Alive and Oliverse.
